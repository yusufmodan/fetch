What's our story?

San Francisco is one of the most dog friendly cities in the country, but there are so many people who aren't allowed to have dogs or simply don't have the time or money. At the same time, there are hundreds of lovable pets waiting to find their forever home that need a friend. Fetch is bringing the two together!

Ready to Fetch? Sign up now! Register your shelter today!

How Fetch Works

We Connect With Shelters

Our mission is centered on connecting dogs in need with people who love dogs. We let local shelters add dogs based on availability.

You Choose Your Activity

We have four different selections for you to make based on the activity you would like your fetch to join you on!

Your Pup Is Delivered

Fetch works with your location to bring you a dog as quickly as we can- usually it will take 15-20 minutes. Once your fetch has arrived, it's playtime for two whole hours!

Bring Back or Adopt!

At the end of your fetch session, you can simply drop off your new friend at the shelter. We know it's hard to say goodbye to these lovable pups, so at the end of every fetch you have the option to Adopt!
